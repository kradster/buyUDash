About Neo
NEO is also called “Chinese Ethereum” or “Ethereum’s killer“ NEO is the first decentralized, open-source cryptocurrency and block chain platform launched in China. NEO has one more crypto-token called GAS (formerly known as ANC-Antcoins). It supports most advanced programming languages like C#, Java and Python. The NEO token is the investment token of the NEO blockchain. It’s hard cap is 100 million tokens which is used in block creation, network management, network changes. One unique thing is that NEO is unlike other cryptocurrencies, it is non- divisible.Neo was launched by the team of Co-Founder Da Hongfei, Core developer – Zhang Zhengwen and Secretary General – Tao Rongqi and other community developers, operators and contributors.
