WHAT IS LOTUS ENERGY



A Lotus Energy Photon (LEP) is a token similar to Lotus Energy Token in the respect that each is created to utilise the combined technologies of the blockchain, distributed ledger technology and smart contracts.

Whereas Lotus Energy Token is ascribed a fiat USD value and is traded on the cryptocurrency exchanges, the Lotus Energy Photons maintain details of energy generated within the blockchain and the distributed ledger.

Lotus Energy Photons enables the trading of the economic value of Lotus Energy and third party energy generators who have joined the Lotus Energy network, to the global community and cryptocurrency mining hubs requiring low-cost energy.

What does the brand name Lotus Energy symbolise in various cultures and to the Lotus Energy Team?

Egyptian Culture:
The Lotus symbol was a potent ancient Egyptian symbol and icon in the mythology and legends of Egypt and often depicted in ancient Egyptian art.

According to ancient Egyptian mythology, the Lotus symbolized the sun, of creation, rebirth and regeneration.

Chinese Culture:
The Lotus is one of the most important flowers in the Chinese culture.

It symbolizes the holy seat of Buddha.

Because the flower rises from the mud and blooms in exquisite beauty, it symbolizes perfection and purity of both the heart and mind.

It also represents long life and honor.

Indian Cultures:
The Lotus is an ancient and polyvalent symbol in Asian culture. Hindus revere it with the gods Vishnu, Brahma and to a lesser degree Kubera, and the goddesses Lakshmi and Saraswati.

Often used as an example of divine beauty and purity, Vishnu is often described as the “Lotus-Eyed One”.

The lotus flower is one of the Ashtamangala of Buddhism, representative of creation and cosmic renewal and “primordial purity”.
