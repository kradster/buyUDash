How To Use Withdrawls/Additional Wallet Funds

Initially the funds in the additional wallet will be in locked status. You need to make them free for use/withdraw.
There are several easy ways you can get them free for use/withdraw-

  • 1
    If you’re not holding any BUC tokens then-
    1. Amount equal to 30% of total amount of fee you pay during trading will get free for use.
    2. Amount equal to 20% of total amount of fee your referred person pay during trading will get free for use.
  • 2
    If you’re holding 1-500 BUC tokens then-
    1. Amount equal to 35% of total amount of fee you pay during trading will get free for use.
    2. Amount equal to 25% of total amount of fee your referred person pay during trading will get free for use.
  • 3
    If you’re holding 501-1500 BUC tokens then-
    1. Amount equal to 40% of total amount of fee you pay during trading will get free for use.
    2. Amount equal to 30% of total amount of fee your referred person pay during trading will get free for use.
  • 4
    If you’re holding 1501-2500 BUC tokens then-
    1. Amount equal to 45% of total amount of fee you pay during trading will get free for use.
    2. Amount equal to 35% of total amount of fee your referred person pay during trading will get free for use.
  • 5
    If you’re holding 2501 and above BUC tokens then-
    1. Amount equal to 50% of total amount of fee you pay during trading will get free for use.
    2. Amount equal to 40% of total amount of fee your referred person pay during trading will get free for use.
